Logistics Cost Audit & Optimization Dashboard for "SwiftMove Logistics"

SwiftMove Logistics was facing rising operations costs, frequent delivery delays, and overtime inefficiencies. The goal was to identify cost leaks and suggest Optimizations.

Objectives

  • Visualize key logistics metrics overtime
  • Identify patterns and problem areas (e.g. overtime, delays)
  • Recommend data-driven improvements

The Dataset

  • Simulated weekly logistics data
  • Metrics: fleet utilization, overtime hours, delivery times, costs, etc.

The Dashboard

  • KPI cards: Total Maintenance Cost, Avg Fleet Utilization, etc.
  • Combo chart to compare Maintenance vs Overtime
  • Trend charts for Overtime, Fuel costs, Delivery Delays
  • Text insight summary with findings and recommendations

Key Insights

  • Week 8 had the worst performance (lowest fleet use, highest delays)
  • Fuel cost/km was rising, likely due to inefficient routing
  • High overtime correlated with increased maintenance expenses

Recommendations

  • Implement route optimization tools
  • Preventive vehicle maintenance
  • Better staff scheduling to reduce overtime
  • Weekly dashboard tracking for operations

Tools Used

  • Power BI
  • Excel (data cleaning)
  • DAX for calculations

Deliverables

  • Power BI Dashboard
  • Excel Data Sheet

Result

A scalable dashboard any logistics team can use to track performance and reduce costs.
