The Senzo Mchunu Scandal Tracker

📌 Project Overview

I wanted to simulate how a project manager, business analyst, or crisis response team would track a high-risk political scandal using agile boards for evidence tracking, stakeholder updates, and risk response.

🔧 Tools Used

  • Trello - Crisis Tracker

📈 Key Outcomes

  • Broke the crisis into categories: timeline, evidence, reactions, investigations, media.
  • Created custom labels to track verified/unverified claims.
  • Simulated formal processes (e.g., Public Protector case, press responses).
  • Used agile principles to manage and move cards across stages.

📂 Deliverables

  • Trello Board

💡 Lessons Learned

This case taught me alot about crisis management, law and politics interplay, evidence-based decison-making, and transparency and intergrity in leadership
