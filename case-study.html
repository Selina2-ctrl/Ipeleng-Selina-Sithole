Bakery Operations Improvement Case Study

I helped a small bakery reduce ingredient stockouts and streamline order tracking using Google Sheets and Trello. The result: fewer missed orders and smoother operations.

Problem

The bakery was struggling with inconsistent stock levels and lost customer orders due to poor tracking and communication.

Solution

  • Created a Google Sheets stock tracker with low-stock alerts.
  • Built a Trello board workflow from "Order Received" to "Completed."
  • Mapped out the entire order-to-delivery process with a clear visual diagram.
